About Nursify

  • What is Nursify?

    Nursify is a mobile device application that connects people who need First Aid Help with volunteers trained to provide First Aid Help.

  • How it works

    With a single button click, people in need or present to an incident can request First Aid Help.

  • What happens next?

    Volunteers receive the notification and, if available, respond to it again with a simple button click declaring that they are on their way.
